## About Me

I'm a Research Scientist specialising in **Computer Vision**, **AI**, and **Robotics** with a PhD in Image Processing & Machine Learning and Postdoctoral research in Computer Vision & Deep Learning.

I work at the intersection of perception, intelligence, and autonomy — building systems that can see, reason, and act in the real world.

- Open to **research collaborations**, **AI consulting**, and **speaking engagements**
- Always exploring the edge of what's possible with vision + language models

Computer Vision          →  Object detection, segmentation, 3D reconstruction

Robotics & Autonomy      →  Perception pipelines, ROS, embedded AI

Image Processing         →  Medical imaging, remote sensing, industrial inspection
